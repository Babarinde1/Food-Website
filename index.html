The Band

The restaurant menu is organized by categories (appetizers, soups, salads, entrees, sides, and drinks) of menu items. The main objective builds the system is to provide ordering and reservation service online to the customer. Each menu item has a name, price, and associated recipe. A recipe for a menu item has a chef, preparation instruction, and associated ingredients. With this system online, ordering and reservation management will become easier and systematic to replace a traditional system where are still using paper. To resister a meal online, the customer has to become a member first then he can access the later part of the site. This project is to facilitate customers for making online ordering and reservations. The option of becoming a member was only an attempt to avoid (to some extent) placing the fake bookings.
